Italian salad

Description

Healthy, tasty and quick to prepare

Ingredients

  • 1 Salad head
  • 2 avocados
  • Olive oil
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 400 g tomatoes
  • 3 dl beluga lentils

Steps

  1. Boil the lentils for 20 minutes
  2. Chop everything else and toss together with the oil and mustard.
  3. Serve with the lentils
  4. Enjoy!
